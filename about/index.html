About

My name is Thiago Mosqueiro, physicist by training, and big fan of rock 'n roll, movies and science (especially together). I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the BioCircuits Institute, University of California San Diego, working under the supervision of Dr. Noa Pinter-Wollman (UCSD), Dr. Brian Smith (ASU) and Dr. Ramon Huerta (UCSD). I graduated in Physics from the University of São Paulo, among the largest universities in the south hemisphere.
